Python on Codecademy

Week 2 of the Mechanical MOOC lurches ahead and I am finding Codecademy very helpful in walking me through small bits of sample code. Picking up a bit of shell scripting beforehand has definitely proven beneficial ... the idea of variables, conditionals, loops and creating functions translates nicely as I figure out Python's syntax.
